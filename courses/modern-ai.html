How to study this track

A practical rhythm so the lessons compound instead of feeling like disconnected theory.

  • Treat this as a systems track, not a prompt track. Think in loops: retrieve, reason, call tools, evaluate, observe, and harden.
  • Work through RAG, agents, and structured outputs before diving into scaling and inference optimization. Reliability comes before throughput.
  • Make evaluation and observability part of the study rhythm. In modern AI, hidden regressions are more expensive than obvious bugs.
  • Use the safety and guardrails lessons alongside production scaling lessons so you build discipline around failure modes, not just features.

What this track gives you

Use this page as the high-level map, then open the highlighted lessons when you want the full breakdown, code, and exercises inside the academy’s lesson engine.

Agents that can actually be trusted

Ops, evals, and safety

Tracing, cost monitoring, custom benchmarks, injection defenses, and structured outputs make modern AI systems measurable and controllable.

Scale and adapt

vLLM, speculative decoding, quantization, LoRA/QLoRA, and fine-tuning paths prepare you for bigger traffic, tighter latency targets, and domain-specific behavior.
